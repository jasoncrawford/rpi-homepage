The Progress Conference is an annual four-day gathering of leaders from technology, policy, academia, and culture, organized by the Roots of Progress Institute.

## Progress Conference 2026

**Dates:** October 8–11, 2026  
**Location:** Lighthaven Campus, Berkeley, California

The third annual Progress Conference will convene approximately 400 leaders for deep conversation, idea exchange, and collaborative project development around human progress.

### Content Tracks

- **Human Talent & Potential** — education, immigration, labor markets
- **AI & Robotics** — autonomous vehicles, manufacturing automation
- **Security & Resilience** — biosecurity, AI safety, supply chains

### Confirmed Speakers

- Adam Brown (Google DeepMind)
- Tyler Cowen (Mercatus Center)
- Dmitri Dolgov (Waymo)
- Michael Kremer (Nobel Laureate, University of Chicago)
- John Martinis (Nobel Laureate, Qolab)
- Scott Wiener (California State Senator)
- Stephen Winchell (DARPA)
- 25+ additional speakers

### Program Structure

**Main Conference (Friday & Saturday)**
- Full-day sessions with keynotes, speaker tracks, and unconference sessions
- Outdoor garden auditorium for plenary sessions
- On-site childcare available

**Optional Add-Ons (Thursday & Sunday)**
- Factory tours of Bay Area deep tech companies
- Interest-specific sessions and unconference time
- Lightning talks and pitch sessions
- Welcome dinner Thursday evening
