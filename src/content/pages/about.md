The Roots of Progress Institute is a nonprofit dedicated to establishing a new philosophy of progress for the 21st century—one that supports innovation, science, and technology as forces for human flourishing.

## Our Mission

We work to build a culture of progress: one that understands where human advancement comes from, values the people and institutions that drive it, and takes ambitious action to accelerate it. Progress is not automatic or inevitable. Our goal is to make it so.

## Our Programs

The Institute runs several programs to advance progress studies as a discipline and build the progress community:

**Fellowship** — The Blog-Building Intensive accelerates careers of progress intellectuals through training, networking, and mentorship. Our fellows produce essays that contribute to the broader conversation about human advancement.

**Annual Conference** — We convene writers, builders, policymakers, and students to exchange ideas and launch collaborative projects. The Progress Conference has grown from 200 attendees in 2024 to over 350 in 2025.

**Progress in Medicine** — A summer career exploration program for high school students interested in medicine, biotechnology, health policy, and longevity fields.

**Online Community** — We maintain the Progress Forum and Progress Studies Slack channel, connecting thousands of people interested in progress studies.
