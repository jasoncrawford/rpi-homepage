Progress Conference 2025 continues to expand as a significant gathering for the progress community, now expecting over 300 attendees.

## Recent Additions

The conference has announced several major updates:

**Speakers:** 21 more great speakers have joined, including representatives from Navier, Arcadia Science, Base Power, Softmax, and California Forever. The speaker roster is now finalized.

**Sponsors:** Seven additional sponsors have committed, including Jane Street, Renaissance Philanthropy, and others. A limited number of sponsorships at $25K and $50K levels remain available.

**Facility Tours:** Five companies—Astranis, Astro Mechanica, Longshot Space, Navier, and Zipline—will host attendee tours during optional Thursday and Sunday programming.

**Applications:** The open application process closed after receiving "more than twice as many applications to attend as last year." Applicants can expect admission decisions by June 30th.

## Community Impact

The organizers reflect on the growing ecosystem: "the progress and abundance community is numbering in the several hundreds thinkers, doers, builders, storytellers, policy makers, and others."

Prior year talks are available on YouTube through Freethink's recording partnership.
