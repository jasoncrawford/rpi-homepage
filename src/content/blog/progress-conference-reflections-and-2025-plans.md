The inaugural Progress Conference, held in October 2024, drew over 200 attendees and garnered exceptional feedback. Participants rated their likelihood of returning at an average 9 out of 10, equivalent to a Net Promoter Score of 68.

## Conference Achievements

The event successfully achieved its primary objective of fostering connections. Survey respondents highlighted several key strengths:

- **Curated attendees:** One participant noted "the people/attendees were expertly curated and very high quality."
- **Venue design:** The location encouraged organic interactions, with one attendee observing, "you would randomly bump into people."
- **Networking impact:** Respondents reported meeting influential figures and forming meaningful connections across the progress community.

The conference generated substantial post-event engagement, with over a dozen write-ups from notable commentators including Noah Smith, Packy McCormick, Scott Alexander, and Bryan Walsh. Social media responses celebrated the gathering as transformative, with attendees describing it as "the best conference I've ever been to."

## 2025 Plans

The organization will host the second annual Progress Conference in October 2025, in the San Francisco Bay Area, continuing partnership with Lighthaven as the venue.

## Job Opening

Roots of Progress is hiring a full-time Event Manager (remote, US-based) to coordinate the annual conference and develop additional community gatherings. The position requires strong project management skills and a passion for community building. Applications close at the end of Q1 2025.
