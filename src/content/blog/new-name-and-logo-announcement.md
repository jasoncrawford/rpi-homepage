The Roots of Progress officially rebranded as the Roots of Progress Institute (RPI) on June 25, 2024. This name change reflects the organization's evolution from a primarily writing-focused endeavor into a full-fledged cultural institute.

The organization retained "Roots" in its name to preserve brand recognition and because the term symbolizes their focus on "the deepest causes of progress, and the fundamental ideas underlying it." The addition of "Institute" positions them appropriately within the broader progress movement.

The rebrand includes a new logo and website designed by And–Now. The logo expands the root concept to include branches and leaves, symbolizing "life, growth, and flourishing." The inverted symmetry represents "the logic, structure, and clarity we aim to bring to the discourse."

## What This Means

Organizational structure clarification includes the original blog continuing at blog.rootsofprogress.org as Jason Crawford's personal outlet, while rootsofprogress.org serves as the institutional home. The Substack newsletter now organizes content into separate sections for essays and official announcements.

The institute highlighted upcoming initiatives including selection of fellows for the 2024 fellowship program and announcement of their first annual progress conference.
