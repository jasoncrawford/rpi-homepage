The Roots of Progress Institute is hosting its third annual conference this fall. Last year's event drew over 350 attendees and received strong praise from participants who called it "one of the best (or the best!) conference they had ever attended."

## Conference Details

**When:** October 8–11, 2026

**Where:** Berkeley, CA at the Lighthaven campus

**Hosts:** Roots of Progress Institute, Abundance Institute, Foresight Institute, Foundation for American Innovation, Human Progress, Institute for Progress, Institute for Humane Studies, and Works in Progress

## Program Structure

The main conference runs Friday and Saturday with speaker presentations covering robotics, education, construction, and other topics. Thursday and Sunday offer optional add-on activities including factory tours and interest group events. Attendees can participate in workshops, run unconference sessions, and network with builders, academics, policymakers, investors, and writers focused on progress.

An initial speaker slate will be announced by April. Previous conferences featured speakers like Sam Altman and Tyler Cowen, with recorded talks available online.

The conference supports the Institute's mission to establish a new progress philosophy for the 21st century and build a culture of progress.
