The progress movement has expanded considerably, spawning various initiatives including journals, research institutions, and educational programs. Related movements have emerged under different banners like the "abundance agenda" and "techno-optimism," all united by a shared vision for scientific, technological, and economic advancement benefiting humanity.

The Roots of Progress Institute is organizing the inaugural Progress Conference 2024, described as "a two-day event to connect people in the progress movement." The gathering aims to facilitate networking, idea exchange, and collaborative project development within the community.

## Event Details

- **Dates:** October 18–19, 2024
- **Location:** Berkeley, CA (Lighthaven campus)
- **Co-hosts:** Roots of Progress Institute, Foresight Institute, HumanProgress.org, Institute for Humane Studies, Institute for Progress, and Works in Progress magazine

**Notable speakers include** Patrick Collison, Tyler Cowen, Jason Crawford, and Steven Pinker, with approximately 20 additional speakers addressing human progress, policy, technology, and media across four programming tracks.

The conference expects over 200 participants representing intellectuals, entrepreneurs, policymakers, and students. While invitation-based, prospective attendees may submit applications through July 15th. Early sponsors include the Cato Institute, Astera Institute, and Freethink Media.
