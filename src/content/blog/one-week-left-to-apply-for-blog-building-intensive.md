The Roots of Progress Blog-Building Intensive is an eight-week program designed for aspiring writers focused on progress-related topics. The 2024 application deadline is Friday, June 7. Successful applicants become Roots of Progress fellows, gaining ongoing support and network access beyond the program itself.

## Program Suitability

The intensive welcomes writers at various career stages. Previous cohort participants included established professionals from think tanks, academia, and major publications. Brian Balkus, who had published in Palladium Magazine, noted the program was "incredibly helpful even as a somewhat more established writer."

Elle Griffin, already commanding over 10,000 Substack subscribers, described it as "life-changing," predicting that "all of the fellows in my program will be famous intellectuals."

## Eligibility Requirements

Applicants should demonstrate:
- A clear vision for progress-focused writing
- Proven writing quality on their chosen topic
- Serious commitment to writing as a career or significant project

Previous publication or large audience are beneficial but not required.

## Specialized Tracks

Two focused tracks are available:

**AI Track:** For writers addressing technical depth, domain expertise across industries (law, science, education), and thoughtful examination of AI risks.

**Heavy Industry Track:** Covering manufacturing, construction, transportation, energy, and defense—fields experiencing recent innovation despite historical stagnation.

## Notable Advisors

The program features prominent figures including Tyler Cowen, Max Roser, and domain-specific experts like Andrej Karpathy (AI) and Blake Scholl (heavy industry).
