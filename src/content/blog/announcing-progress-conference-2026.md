The Roots of Progress Institute has announced the third annual Progress Conference, scheduled for October 8–11, 2026, in Berkeley, California.

## Event Details

**Location:** Lighthaven campus in Berkeley, CA

**Dates:** October 8–11, 2026

**Expected Attendance:** Approximately 400 leaders from technology, policy, and cultural sectors

## Keynote Speakers

The conference will feature several prominent speakers, including:

- Dmitri Dolgov (Co-CEO, Waymo)
- Tyler Cowen (Mercatus Center)
- Stephen Winchell (Director, DARPA)
- John Martinis (Nobel Laureate in Physics)
- Michael Kremer (Nobel Laureate in Economics)

Additionally, "20+ additional speakers will share ideas across three tracks: Human Talent & Potential, AI & Robotics, and Security & Resilience."

## Application Process

The event operates on an invitation-only basis, though anyone may apply. Interested parties can complete the open application by May 31st.

## Program Structure

The main conference occurs Friday and Saturday, with optional Thursday and Sunday activities including factory tours and interest group gatherings. Childcare with progress-themed programming will be available.

## Host Organizations

Seven organizations are co-hosting the event, including the Roots of Progress Institute, Abundance Institute, and Foresight Institute.
